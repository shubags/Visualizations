Project Data Set Description and Motivation

This data set consists of military spending by various countries over a period of time

  • For NATO countries, the data is available for the past 65 years
  • For non-NATO countries, the data is available for the past 26 years

The data is shown in terms of U.S. dollars, local currency, and as a percentage of GDP.

Motivation (B-Level)

I spent many years focused on analyzing the global defense industry. I would like to create a visualization of some of the data I did analysis on by never really visualized. I have linked the defense spending, by country, by year, but I intend to combine this with GDP data from the IMF. There are interesting trends about how certain countries have increased spending as a percentage of GDB while others have significantly decreased spending.
